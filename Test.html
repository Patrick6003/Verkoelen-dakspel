How to Play

Protect the magical wand from pirates by casting spells! Use the left and right arrow keys to rotate the wand and the spacebar to shoot spells (or use the on-screen buttons on mobile). Defeat pirates to earn points: regular pirates (10 points), blue fat pirates with stripes (20 points), red pirate captains with hooks (50 points), and green crocodiles (100 points). You start with 3 lives. The game gets harder as you level up. Survive as long as you can!
